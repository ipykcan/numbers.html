Types Of Numbers

Natural Numbers

Natural numbers are all positive integers from 1 to infinity. Natural numbers do not include 0 or negative numbers.

Integers

An integer is the number zero, a positive natural number, or the negation of a positive natural number.

Rational Numbers

A rational number is any number that can be written as a fraction, where both the numerator (the top number) and the denominator (the bottom number) are integers, and the denominator is not equal to zero.

Irrational Numbers

An irrational number is a real number that cannot be expressed as a ratio of integers
